Why delet?

"Why should I be using delet? I already have enough Discord bots!"
Well, delet is no ordinary Discord bot. It encompasses a range of useful features, as well as the ability to feel comfortable and at-home using delet, through features such as customisable languages (soon™) and currencies.

But literally, why delet?

The term "delet" comes from the delet this meme. However, delet was chosen as the name for this bot due to it being both fun and fitting for its original, single purpose.
